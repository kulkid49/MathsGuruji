Frequently Asked Questions

Can the subscription be cancelled?

Yes, the subscription can be cancelled anytime from the Parent Zone in the app or your Play Store account.

What is the cost of subscribing to Math Guruji programs?

Our subscription cost varies by program. Please visit our pricing section or contact support for the latest plans.

How many profiles can I add on the Disney. Math Guruji Early Learn App?

You can add up to 3 child profiles per subscription on the Early Learn App.

Do you provide customer support?

Yes, our customer support is available via email, chat, and phone to assist with any issues or inquiries.

Can I access the content on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in on multiple devices, but only one device can stream content at a time.

Is the subscription auto-renewed?

Yes, subscriptions are auto-renewed unless cancelled before the billing cycle ends.

Do I need internet to use the app?

Internet is required to stream content. Some content may be available for offline viewing if downloaded.

What kind of placement support will be given post completion of program?

We provide placement assistance, including resume building, interview preparation, and connecting you with potential employers.

What are the accepted payment methods?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular wallets.

Can I transfer my subscription to someone else?

Subscriptions are non-transferable for security and policy reasons.
